### Barronite Miner

Mines barronite in the Cam Torum / dwarven area, with optional smithing of deposits at the anvil and banking.

## Features
- Mine barronite rocks
- Walk to anvil to process deposits
- Bank / deposit handling
- Keeps hammer + barronite shards by default
- XP and shard tracking

## Requirements
- Access to the barronite mining area
- Hammer for processing deposits (kept in inventory/bank)
- Suitable Mining level for barronite

## Tips
- Adjust `ITEMSTOKEEP` if you want to keep extra drops
- Experimental walker is enabled by default
