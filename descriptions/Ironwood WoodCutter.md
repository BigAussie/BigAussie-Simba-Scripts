### Ironwood Woodcutter

Chops ironwood trees with banking or powerchop, nest pickup, and optional world hopping.

## Features
- Multi-tree chopping with special attack support
- Bank logs or powerchop (drop)
- Bird nest pickup
- Prefer populated trees option
- Custom break override
- World hopping on an interval
- Discord hourly + session summary webhooks
- On-screen progress / profit tracking

## Requirements
- Access to ironwood trees
- Suitable Woodcutting level and axe
- Bank nearby if not powerchopping

## Tips
- Configure hopping, breaks, and webhooks in the GUI
- Enable nest pickup if you want nests kept
