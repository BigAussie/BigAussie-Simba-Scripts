### Sorceress's Garden (Sq'irk)

Runs Sorceress's Garden for summer sq'irks — pick, crush to juice, and hand in for Thieving XP.

## Features
- Enter garden / pick sq'irks
- Make juice with pestle & mortar + beer glass
- Hand in to Osman / Selim flow
- Banking for supplies
- Energy / run restore handling (won't drink sq'irk juice as energy)

## Requirements
- Sorceress's Garden unlocked
- Pestle and mortar + beer glasses in bank
- Suitable Thieving level for summer garden

## Tips
- Keep energy items in bank if you want automatic restores
- Start with glasses and pestle available
