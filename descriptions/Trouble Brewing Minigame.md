### Trouble Brewing

Plays Trouble Brewing for Pieces of Eight — woodcutting, water, fletching, and hopper filling.

## Features
- Join red (north) or blue (south) team
- Woodcutting / scrapey bark flow
- Fill water buckets and hoppers
- Optional power-fletching
- Tracks Pieces of Eight and resources
- Discord webhook options

## Requirements
- Trouble Brewing unlocked (Cabin Fever)
- Suitable gear for the minigame island
- Start at / able to reach the Trouble Brewing lobby

## Tips
- Pick your team and method in the GUI before starting
- Power-fletching is optional for alternate XP focus
