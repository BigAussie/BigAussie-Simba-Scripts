### Salt Miner

Mines Weiß / salt rocks and can craft **stony** / **icy basalt** from basalt + salts.

## Features
- Mine basalt, efh / te / urt salts
- Optional basalt-only mode
- Craft stony & icy basalt to configured amounts
- Banking / noting support
- GUI-driven targets for each resource

## Requirements
- Access to the salt mine (Weiss)
- Suitable Mining level
- Space for salts / basalt in inventory and bank

## Tips
- Set desired salt and basalt craft amounts in the GUI
- Use basalt-only if you do not need salt grinding
