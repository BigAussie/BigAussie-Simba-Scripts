### Ardougne Superheat Iron

Mines iron at Ardougne, then **Superheats** ore into iron bars for Magic + Smithing XP.

## Features
- Mine → Superheat → bank loop
- Tracks bars made and XP
- WaspLib antiban

## Requirements
- **Superheat Item** unlocked (Magic)
- Nature runes + fire-capable staff / fire runes
- Access to Ardougne iron rocks and a nearby bank
- Ardougne cloak / diary teleports help but are not strictly required depending on setup

## Tips
- Start near the Ardougne mining area with runes ready
- Keep nature runes stocked in bank
