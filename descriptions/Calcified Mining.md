### Calcified Mining

Mines calcified rocks in Cam Torum, with deposit processing and banking options.

## Features
- Mine calcified rocks (optional ignore waterfalls)
- Process deposits at the blacksmith / anvil area
- Power-mining mode (drop)
- Banking support
- Tracks shards, moths, and XP
- Discord webhook on terminate

## Requirements
- Access to Cam Torum mining sites
- Hammer for processing deposits
- Suitable Mining level

## Tips
- Use the GUI for power-mining, waterfall ignore, and webhook settings
- Start with a hammer available in inventory or bank
